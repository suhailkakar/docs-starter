## How to Use

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Getting Tokens">
    - Connect your Ethereum wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
    - Ensure you're on the Saint Petersburg testnet (Chain ID: 2391)
    - Request testnet TAC tokens for gas fees
    - Tokens are delivered instantly to your wallet
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Rate Limits">
    - Reasonable rate limits to prevent abuse
    - Multiple requests allowed per day for active development
    - Fair distribution ensures availability for all developers
    - Contact support if you need additional tokens for large projects
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
